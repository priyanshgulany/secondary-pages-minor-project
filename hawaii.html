ROUND THE WORLD : HAWAII TOUR DETAILS
"Kauai"

Kauai driving tours offer that local knowledge for your entire vacation. And they’ve even put together itineraries that take the hard work out of planning your trip. The tours will give you suggested times, but you’re in complete control: choose your own pace, bypass some locations and spend extra time at others. It’s like having a local guide with you, and all the insider information you could hope to receive. Shaka Guide has four Kauai itineraries available, so let’s take a look at an unforgettable four-day tour of the gorgeous Garden Isle.

"Waimea and Na Pali"

spans 6 to 8 hours and starts off the right way — with a stop at Kauai Coffee Farm. Between there and the first Waimea Canyon lookout, you’ll learn about some great stops that you might otherwise miss. Find the uniquely beautiful Red Dirt Waterfall, and take a short hike to some awesome canyon views. Later in the tour, you’ll find yourself at two different spots with views of the stunning Na Pali Coast. Stops in Waimea Town and Hanapepe offer food, shopping and relaxation. Shaka Guide offers info on the rich cultural history of these places and more.

"North Shore Kauai"

The tour takes 6 to 8 hours, but we wouldn’t blame you for spending some extra time at one of the day’s many beaches. Lumahai Beach is a fantastic place to relax, and Tunnels is excellent for snorkeling.
